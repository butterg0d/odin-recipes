Thai Tea with Cold Foam

This is a recipe for Cold foam which can be added to beverages such as coffee, milk, and desserts.

Ingredients

    60ml Milk
    120ml Heavy Cream
    60ml Cream Cheese
    30ml Sugar

Steps

    Mix cream cheese and sugar thoroughly
    Add milk, heavy cream into mixture and mix thoroughly
    Once consistent, use a frother to build-up
